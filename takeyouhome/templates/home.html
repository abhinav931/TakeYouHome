Vision

COVID-19 a massive Pandemic that has swept across Humanity. Our country is facing a national lockdown from 40+ days and has chances to continue even longer. We all are facing this, but facing together in our homes with our families.But what about those who are Stuck in a city far from their home town? Our Citizens are entrapped with minimal essentials and lacking necessities, also Health amenities. Such exposure to major risks might worth their lives. We here have taken the initiative to help our government, because each support will save our nation. The idea focuses upon the people Stuck in difficult circumstances in any State or District and are unable to reach their home towns. Our designed website will provide the data of citizens in need along with all their details so as to make it easier to locate for the State Government & further to take action upon. This will reduce people migrating to their villages covering distance on foot that increases the risk of Covid-19. A conveyance to send them home would be more safe and reliable, also they'll be relieved. The data of people will be highly authentic to avoid any redundancies. The authorities of Gram panchayat, collectorate will be needed to serve for the well being in this threat. We consider it our duty and will work towards it with the Support of government together. For the People, By the People, Of the People.
